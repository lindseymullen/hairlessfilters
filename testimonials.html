Pros of Using Hair Less Filters

  • Hair doesn't normally stick to filters, if it does a quick brush with your fingers will remove it!
  • Clean the Hair Less filter once a week with a damp cloth without ever removing it from the blower.
  • Deep clean every 6 months by taking the Hair Less filter off and running it under water. This takes around 10 minutes and soap should never be used.
  • Most groomers say they have not replaced the filters that come with their blower or cleaned them since installing the Hair Less Filter.
  • Groomers using Hair Less Filters with their Shop-Vacs all say "Wow", no more daily cleaning of the pleats!
  • The Shop-Vac filters normally last 18 months but with the Hair Less Filters they last at least 6 years!
  • The equipment that have Hair Less Filters installd have better air flow, run cooler, and go longer between repairs.
